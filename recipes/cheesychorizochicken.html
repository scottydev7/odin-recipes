Cheesy Chorizo Chicken

Home Page

A one pan wonder with lots of cheesy goodness!

This is a great one that can be made in under 10 minutes and is a real crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Coconut Oil
  • Sliced Chorizo
  • Red Onion
  • Mozerella
  • Diced Chicken
  • Spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add the coconut oil to a pan
  2. Fry up the red onion and chorizo for 1 minute
  3. Add the diced chicken and cook for 3 minutes until chicken is white
  4. Lower temperature and in the spinach stirring regularly to wilt it down
  5. Add the chopped mozerella and bring off the heat
  6. Serve as is or with a side of rice
