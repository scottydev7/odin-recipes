A quick and easy one

This is a super speedy one that can be made in under 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut Oil
  • Sliced Chorizo
  • Red Onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Add the coconut oil to a pan
  2. Fry up the red onion and chorizo for 1 minute
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and simmer for 3 minutes
  4. Create 2 wells in the pan and crack an egg into each
  5. Sprinkle the feta over top of the eggs
  6. Cover the pan and leave on a low heat for 5 minutes
  7. Serve as is or with a side of pasta/rice
